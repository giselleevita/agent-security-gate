# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors must communicate professionally, disagree on technical grounds, respect
privacy, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or publication of another
person's private information.

## Scope

This standard applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and
other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method in `SECURITY.md`. The
maintainer may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when behavior is harmful or unprofessional.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, impact, and repeated behavior.
