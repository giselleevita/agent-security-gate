## About the job

![](https://media.jobs.ch/images/46864599-6e7b-4bd8-bbde-f7358b113e13?format=jpg&dimensions=1200x400&transform=crop)

**Your contribution to KPMG**

- Get to know "Thought Leadership" firsthand responding to today's changing market environment and new challenges in cyber security
- Help our clients assess, build, and improve the effectiveness and quality of their information security as part of interdisciplinary teams of specialists working on advisory projects
- Analyze complex enterprise environments from a cyber security perspective in different industries
- Provide visibility and remediation of vulnerabilities and compromises, by among other executing penetrations tests and supporting crisis simulations
- Help clients effectively and efficiently respond to cyber incidents
- Assess information security management systems and provide independent assurance in accordance with national and international regulations and standards

**This is what makes you successful**

- Bachelor's or Master's degree in (business)informatics, computer science, cyber security or similar
- First experience in information security or in a comparable environment is preferred
- A must: an interest in advisory topics and a deep technical understanding gained through training or other experience
- Team spirit, determination, willingness to take action, and quick and analytical thinking
- Proficiency in English and German
