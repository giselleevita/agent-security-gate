# Work for Switch

In our exciting work environment, we connect universities, the free market economy, research and services. We have been operating and protecting the .ch and .li domains and other critical digital infrastructure since the early days of the internet. Experienced specialists in the areas of cyber security, digital identities and the use of research data work hand in hand here. With our expertise, together we facilitate and accelerate digital developments for the Swiss education, research and innovation community and other customers.

We are constantly growing and looking for new talent. You can expect exciting and meaningful tasks with plenty of room for creativity here. We also offer attractive opportunities for personal development – both on and off the job. Join our team and help us to shape the digital services of the future!

Find vacancies now

We are looking for bright people with initiative and a passion for quality. We believe that the ability to work independently and to cooperate as part of an interdisciplinary team is just as important as maintaining respectful relationships with colleagues. Are you ready for a new, exciting challenge? Then we look forward to receiving your application!

## The benefits for you

We offer you numerous perks and opportunities for further development. We invest in you and your skills! Because you help us to ensure we are well positioned as an organisation and allow us to support our customers in the best possible way.

### Attractive working models

Work in a way that suits you. We offer you part-time models, the opportunity to work from home, flexible working hours, full compensation for overtime and the option to work abroad for a limited period. You can also take up to six months of unpaid leave by arrangement.

### Personal development

We support your continuous development, whether it's «on the job», «off the job», or through attending conferences. We also contribute towards the costs of courses and/or the time you need for your further education. A temporary reduction in your working hours is also possible.

### Flexible pensions

Choose the pension fund model that best suits your current life situation. You can switch to another model at the end of each year without incurring additional costs.

### Parental leave on 100% pay

We offer female employees 16 weeks and male employees two weeks of parental leave on full pay.

### Subsidised meals

If you work full-time, you get up to CHF 180 per month for your meals with the Lunchcheck card.

### Private accident insurance

We take out private accident insurance for you. The costs of outpatient and inpatient treatment in a private ward are covered – all over the world.

### Internet and public transport

We also contribute towards your home office internet costs and public transport travelcard. And speaking of the latter, your workplace is super centrally located and easily accessible by public transport.

### Leave

We offer you five weeks of leave, increasing to six weeks from the age of 50. Help us fill vacancies and we’ll reward you with a bonus.

### Free beverages

Enjoy the free drinks on offer when you take a break in our modern cafeteria.

## Your career at Switch

We offer talented young people several ways of gaining professional experience. For example, you can start your professional life here with a part-time job or an internship. You get to organise your working hours in the way that best fits your studies. And after finishing your training, you can apply for a junior position or complete your ICT apprenticeship with us.

### Apprenticeship in computer science

Do you want to plan networks, implement software solutions, put server systems into operation and handle complex support requests? Then apply to us for a four-year apprenticeship as an EFZ computer scientist – specialising in application or platform development. During your apprenticeship, you will get to know various departments and be supervised by motivated vocational trainers. You will complete the basic apprenticeship year at the vocational training centre. We are looking for young people with good grades in mathematics and science (Sek A level). Logical thinking, creativity and a high level of concentration are also very important for this apprenticeship. We organise career exploration days several times a year and publish open apprenticeships on our job portal.

### IT internship

An internship gives you an-depth insight into our work and allows you to get to know our clients and the range of services we offer. Our internships usually last six to 12 months. Part-time options are also available. Experienced employees are on hand to coach you. Do you have outstanding initiative and enthusiasm for learning? Then we look forward to meeting you! We regularly advertise the following vacancies: DevOps Engineer, Software Engineer, Site Reliability Engineer, Security Engineer, Network Engineer.

### Junior IT position

Students and junior professionals with up to three years of work experience can quickly acquire extensive expertise here – both on and off the job. We’re always on the lookout for talented computer science, business information systems and electrical engineering students to fill positions such as Junior Network Engineer, Security Engineer or System Administrator.

We offer committed young people interesting opportunities for promotion: shoulder more responsibility or even take ownership of a project if you’re looking for a new challenge. In addition to numerous benefits, you also enjoy a healthy work-life balance here.

## Apply in three steps

### Send us your application

Apply for your chosen advertised position using our website. Upload your cover letter and CV as well as other relevant documents as PDF files to our platform.

### Look out for our invite

We will review your application and invite suitable applicants for an initial interview. If you and we are interested in working together, there will be a second round of interviews.

### Sign the contract

If we all believe that we’d like to work together in the future, we clarify the last unanswered questions. Then you sign your employment contract – welcome to Switch!
